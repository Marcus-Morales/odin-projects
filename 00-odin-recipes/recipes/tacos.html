Tacos Recipes

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish made from a folded or rolled tortilla filled with a variety of ingredients such as seasoned meat, beans, cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, and salsa. They can be made with either soft or hard tortillas and are typically garnished with sour cream, guacamole, and hot sauce. Tacos are known for their versatility and wide range of flavors.

Ingredients

  • Tacos
  • Beef or Chicken
  • Lettuce and Tomato

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or chicken over medium heat until fully cooked. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the taco seasoning mix and water to the skillet. Stir to combine and simmer for 5 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat or in the microwave for about 30 seconds.
  4. Fill each tortilla with a scoop of the seasoned meat mixture.
  5. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and shredded cheddar cheese.
  6. Add a dollop of sour cream on top.
  7. Serve with salsa and guacamole on the side.
